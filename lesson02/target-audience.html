Target audience: The target audience for this website will be middle-aged adults and senior citizens.

Demographics:

  • 30 & 31 years old.
  • Married
  • One newborn

Goals and tasks:

  • They are struggling to keep their finances in order. They are doing everything they can to stay afloat in life and an unforeseen rainy day might seem trivial to some, but to them it could add another rough day.

Environment:

  • They are comfortable using techonlogy and a simple app/website is something that they can handle with confidence.
