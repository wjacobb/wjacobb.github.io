Welcome to the coast of Cozumel! Here we rent out scooters, ATV's and Jeep's ranging from 1-4 total passengers. We keep our vehicles in good shape to make things as pleasant for you as possible. We hope you enjoy your time in Cozumel as we provide a means of transportation for you as well as others.

We have locations in both near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in el Centro de Cozumel and the other is near the Terminal Peurta Maya. We cater to locals, walk-in tourists, enthusiasts, and cruises.
